# üí° Tips for Presentation

1. **Speak slowly** - Clear speech is important
2. **Point to screen** - Show features when talking about them
3. **Eye contact** - Look at audience, not just screen
4. **Practice transition** - Member 1 should hand over to Member 2 smoothly
5. **Keep backup** - Have notes on phone if you forget
6. **Breathe** - Take small pauses between slides
